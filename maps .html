Here is some Hospitals in Jordan

Al Khalidi Hospital

Al Khalidi Hospital and Medical Center is one of Jordan's oldest and most advanced hospitals.

Jordan Hospital

Jordan Hospital and Medical Center is a 350-bed tertiary academic hospital. It is the largest private academic hospital in Jordan.

Shmaisani Hospital

Shmaisani hospital is one of modern pioneers' hospitals in Jordan that willing to introduce special medical services.

Amman Hospital

The hospital treats patients coming from inside Jordan and abroad including Arabs and other nationalities.

The Specialty Hospital

The Specialty Hospital is a leading private teaching hospital, located in Amman Jordan. It is fully equipped to provide full range of in-patient and out-patient diagnostic.
